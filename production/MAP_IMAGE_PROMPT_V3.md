## PROMPT (wklej do generatora 1:1)

Top-down circular RPG world map. Pixel art style, 64-bit quality (think PS1/N64 era pixel art with rich color depth, detailed shading, and fine dithering). Dark fantasy aesthetic with vibrant glowing accents. Isometric tilt approximately 15 degrees.

---

OVERALL SHAPE:
The map is a large RING/OVAL shape. The center is a void — a dark swirling purple-black abyss, like a portal collapsing inward. Visible cracks in reality radiate from the center. The ring path itself is a worn ancient cobblestone road, wide enough to clearly show branching paths.

---

PLAYER START: positioned at TOP (12:30 clock position — slightly right of 12 o'clock). Mark with a bright white pennant flag labeled "START" in pixel font. A glowing white arrow curves clockwise showing the intended direction of travel.

FINAL BOSS NODE: positioned at TOP-LEFT (11:30 clock position — slightly left of 12 o'clock). This is VERY CLOSE to START but on the OTHER SIDE — going counter-clockwise from start leads here almost immediately. It is the most dangerous shortcut. Design: massive Flying Spaghetti Monster pixel art creature — enormous chaotic golden-brown noodle mass, two enormous glowing red meatball eyes with slit pupils, surrounded by a massive ominous purple-black corruption aura that bleeds visually into adjacent arcs. This is the LARGEST node on the entire map. Above it: a glowing skull-and-crossbones icon with noodle arms. A faint "?" mark glows above the aura, hinting at mystery.

ARC LAYOUT (clockwise from START at 12:30):

ARC 1 — NORDIC (12:30 to ~4 o'clock, top-right quarter):
This is the FIRST arc players enter going clockwise. It should look the most accessible but still dangerous.
Environment: shattered ice cliffs, fragments of a massive frozen tree (Yggdrasil roots cracking through the stone path), Viking longhouse ruins with lit torches, frozen waterfall in background. Snowflakes drifting. Color palette: deep steel blue, silver frost, pale cyan, dark navy.
ARC BOSS NODE (at ~4 o'clock, end of arc): Large CROWN icon, ICY BLUE color. Label underneath: "NORDIC WARLORD". The crown should look like a broken Viking horned helmet rendered as a pixel crown. Boss aura: cold blue frost ring.

ARC 2 — EGYPTIAN (4 o'clock to ~7 o'clock, right-bottom quarter):
Environment: broken pyramid spires rising from the path edges, floating hieroglyph tablets, ghostly semi-transparent figures walking alongside the road (Shade units), golden sand particles drifting upward. Color palette: warm gold, deep amber, sand yellow, shadow purple.
ARC BOSS NODE (at ~7 o'clock): Large CROWN icon, GOLD color. Label: "DUAT GUARDIAN". Crown looks like an Egyptian pharaoh crown (double crown / pschent) in pixel art. Boss aura: golden shimmer with ghostly wisps.

ARC 3 — JAPANESE/MESOAMERICAN (7 o'clock to ~10 o'clock, bottom-left quarter):
Environment: crumbling torii gate archway spanning the path, stone Aztec calendar carvings embedded in the road, ritual torches burning GREEN flame, jade stone walls, autumn red leaves mixed with copal smoke. Color palette: deep crimson, forest green, dark jade, bone white.
ARC BOSS NODE (at ~10 o'clock): Large CROWN icon, CRIMSON-GREEN split color. Label: "RITUAL KING". Crown combines Japanese oni mask with Aztec feather headdress. Boss aura: green fire ring.

ARC 4 — TRANSFORMER/VOID (10 o'clock to 11:30, top-left, small arc before Final Boss):
This arc is SHORT — only 3-4 nodes between the Japanese boss and the Final Boss.
Environment: broken reality — half-formed structures that simultaneously look like multiple things. Floating mirror shards reflecting impossible reflections. Colors bleed between all palettes chaotically. Color palette: shifting iridescent, teal, magenta, fractured rainbow.
No arc boss — this arc leads directly to the Final Boss.

BRANCHING PATHS — IMPORTANT:
The road is NOT a single track. At multiple points along the ring, the path SPLITS into 2 branches that rejoin further along. Show at least 3-4 visible branch points across the ring. Each branch has different node types (e.g., one branch has a shop + combat, other branch has camp + loot). The branches are clearly drawn as separate stone paths that diverge and converge. This gives the player meaningful route choices within each arc.

STYLE REQUIREMENTS:
- 64-bit pixel art quality (PS1/N64 era fidelity — rich color depth, smooth dithering, detailed sprites)
- Background: #06060f (near black with slight blue tint)
- Ring road: worn ancient cobblestone, thick enough to be clearly readable
- All node icons: glowing halos matching their element color
- Overall feel: ancient dying world where mythologies collide and collapse
- No modern flat UI — everything looks carved, painted, or engraved
- The image should feel EPIC and atmospheric, like an old-school RPG world map screen
- Strong contrast between the bright glowing path and the dark void center
- Color bleed between arcs at their borders — they don't end sharply, they bleed into each other
